Overview

Blackbox Preset Builder is a dedicated tool for the 1010music Blackbox that turns preset creation into a clear, visual, and fast workflow. No more hand-editing preset.xml files: import your samples, fine-tune parameters, audition changes instantly, and save clean presets ready for your projects.

Key features
Sound & Pad Editing

Everything you need to shape your sounds directly from the editor before they ever hit the Blackbox.

  • 4×4 pad grid: assign a sample per pad, trigger with a click, play via the virtual keyboard or any MIDI controller.
  • Instant waveform view: quick peak display with Start/End and Loop Start/End markers, plus loop mode indicator (none / forward / bidirectional).
  • Move start, end, and loop points directly in the waveform: drag the markers to trim your sample or adjust the loop in real time while you listen.
  • Full pad editing: gain (dB), pan, pitch (±24 semitones), pad mode (Sample / Clip / Slicer / Granular), Poly / Mono, root note and playback options.
  • Keyboard & MIDI input: play the current pad across the keyboard (with transposition) and test mappings instantly with your MIDI controller.
Audio Editor

Edit the audio assigned to the currently selected pad using a fully non-destructive workflow, with complete Undo / Redo support.

  • Apply common processes instantly: Normalize, Silence, Fade In / Fade Out, Gain, Reverse, DC Offset Removal, and more.
  • Normalize Each Slice helps keep slice-based kits consistent.
  • Pitch / Time lets you adjust tuning or timing while keeping your workflow fast and reversible.
  • All edits are tracked and can be reverted at any time via Undo / Redo.
Workflow & Navigation

Designed to speed up everyday tasks: browsing, assigning samples, reorganizing pads, and preparing presets for the Blackbox.

  • Drag-and-drop everywhere: from file browser to pad to assign a WAV, and between pads to move or copy cells.
  • Smart preview: auto-play option when selecting WAVs in the browser for quick sample auditioning.
  • Fast file browsing: optimized cache for smoother navigation and quicker folder switching.
  • Clear pad overview: see at a glance which pads are used, how they’re configured, and which samples they reference.
Multi-samples (auto-mapping)

Drop a folder of WAV files onto a pad and Blackbox Preset Builder auto-maps the instrument for you, generating all the required Blackbox zones.

  • Reads the root note from WAV metadata when available.
  • Falls back to root note detection from filenames (e.g. Piano-C3.wav, Kick-36.wav).
  • Detects velocity layers when velocity is included in the filename (e.g. Piano-C3-80.wav).
  • Builds proper key ranges and velocity ranges automatically.
  • Uses smart alphabetical auto-mapping when no pitch information is available.
Procedural Generator

The Procedural Generator gives instant inspiration and lets you reshape, randomize or drop new sounds onto any pad in seconds.

  • Generate fully-formed sounds: kicks, basses, synth tones, and more.
  • Drag-and-drop directly onto any pad to update it instantly.
  • Tweak parameters to refine or randomize the result as much as you like.
  • Ideal for sound design sessions or rapidly prototyping new Blackbox presets.
Export & Sharing

Keep your presets portable: move easily between the Blackbox, your DAW, and software samplers.

  • MIDI export for sequences: generate standard .mid files from your patterns to edit, arrange, or layer them inside any DAW.
  • SFZ export for pads: export your pad rack as a single SFZ file, or export individual pads (including multi-samples) as separate SFZ instruments for use in compatible software samplers.
